# Compliance & Audit Requirements

## **Regulatory Compliance**

### **GDPR/CCPA Requirements**
- **Data Minimization**: Log only necessary information
- **Right to Erasure**: Ability to remove user-specific logs
- **Pseudonymization**: Hash/anonymize user identifiers

### **SOX/Financial Compliance**
- **Immutable Logs**: Write-once, tamper-evident log storage
- **Audit Trails**: Complete record of financial transactions
- **Access Controls**: Restricted access to financial operation logs
